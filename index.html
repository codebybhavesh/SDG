About Shree Durvankur Greens

Your trusted partner in green living since 2010

Our Passion

Shree Durvankur Greens is built on a deep passion for plants and a commitment to nurturing greener, healthier spaces. Established in 2010 as a family-run nursery, we have grown into a trusted destination for plant lovers, gardeners, and landscape enthusiasts.

Quality Care

We believe that plants have the power to transform not just spaces, but lives. Every plant at Shree Durvankur Greens is carefully selected, grown, and nurtured with attention and expertise to ensure optimal health and long-lasting growth.

Sustainable Practices

Sustainability lies at the heart of everything we do. By adopting eco-friendly practices and responsible growing methods, we strive to contribute to a greener tomorrow while delivering the highest quality plants to our customers.
