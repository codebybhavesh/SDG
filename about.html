Our Green Journey

Nurturing nature's beauty since 2010 with passion and dedication

Our Story

Shree Durvankur Greens was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring the beauty of nature into people's lives. What started as a small family nursery has grown into a beloved destination for plant enthusiasts across the region.

Our journey began with a passion for plants and a commitment to sustainable practices. Over the years, we've cultivated not just plants, but also lasting relationships with our community of plant lovers.

Today, we take pride in offering a wide variety of indoor and outdoor plants, expert gardening advice, and exceptional customer service to help you create your own green oasis.

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality plants while promoting sustainable gardening practices and fostering a deeper connection between people and nature. We believe in responsible sourcing, eco-friendly growing methods, and educating our community about the benefits of green living.

Our Vision

To create a greener, healthier world by making quality plants accessible to everyone and inspiring a love for gardening in future generations. We envision communities where nature and urban living exist in harmony, and where every home has its own green sanctuary.
